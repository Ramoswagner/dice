DICE FRAMEWORK GUIDE
The Hard Side of Change Management

Developed by Boston Consulting Group (BCG) and published in Harvard Business Review (2005), the DICE model analyzes transformation programs through four critical variables: Duration, Integrity, Commitment, and Effort.

Based on 225 change initiatives across industries, the research revealed that projects scoring 7-13 have high success probability, 14-17 require attention, and 18-28 indicate critical risk.

The formula DICE = D + 2I + 2C1 + C2 + E provides a quantifiable, predictive measure of transformation success.
